Welcome to the home of the Petters Aerosol Group within the department of Chemical and Environmental Engineering at the University of California, Riverside. We study the physiccal and chemical properties of particles between 0.002 and 5 µm with a focus on understanding phase transitions. Our work spans instrument development, laboratory measurements, field observations, and process-level model development.

Please contact me if you are interested in getting involved in research. We are currently looking for motivated PhD students and postdocs to join the team.
