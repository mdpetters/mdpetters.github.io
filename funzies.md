# Nature
- [x] [Heart Rock Trail](https://www.theevolista.com/heart-rock-trail/)
> 50 min drive from Bearloft
> Hiking in San Bernadino National Forest. Park at Camp Seely. Nice 1hr hike through along a river through dense forest.

- [ ] [Joshua Tree National Park](https://www.nps.gov/jotr/index.htm)
> 1.5 hr drive from Bearloft.
> Two distinct desert ecosystems, the Mojave and the Colorado, come together in Joshua Tree National Park.

- [ ] [Sequoia National Park](https://www.nps.gov/seki/index.htm)
> 4 hr drive from Bearloft.
> Huge mountains, rugged foothills, deep canyons, vast caverns, and the world’s largest trees.

- [ ] [Channel Island National Park](https://www.nps.gov/chis/index.htm)
> Boat rides from Ventura Harbor (~2hr from Bearloft). Cost of ride is < $100/person. [Tickets](https://islandpackers.com/book/ventura/trips/) 
> Channel Islands National Park encompasses five remarkable islands and their ocean environment

# Trips
- [x] [Palm Springs Areal Tramway](https://pstramway.com/)
> Hiking at 8000 ft within the San Jacinto State Park/Wilderness are animal friendly. Fun ride and nice visitor center with view over the Coachella valley.
> Has nighttime events and fine dining on the top of the mountain.

- [x] [Laguna Beach Summer Arts Festival and Beach](https://sawdustartfestival.org/)
> Great family friendly beach. Has trolley ride and remote parking ($15). Art festival goes all summer. Cost is moderate.

- [x] [Santa Monica Playhouse](https://www.santamonicaplayhouse.com/) and [Santa Monica Pier](https://www.santamonicapier.org/)
> Fun low-key live theater. Pier has rides and candy. Pacific beach.

- [x] [Griffith Observatory](https://griffithobservatory.org/)
> Fun hike and nice free museum. Check for events (planet viewing). Griffith Park has other seasonal events, e.g. Shakespeare in the Park.
> About 1 hr show on rotating schedule. Need tickets in advance

- [x] [Grunion Run](https://wildlife.ca.gov/Fishing/Ocean/Grunion) and [San Pedro Aquarium](https://cma.recreation.parks.lacity.gov/)
> Nice tidal flats by LB port. Cheap parking, free admission to aquarium. Grunions may or may not come. San Pedro has a free Trolley system to go downtown and back.

- [x] [Little Tokyo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Little_Tokyo,_Los_Angeles) watch out for [Nisei Festival](https://niseiweek.org/) in August
> Park at 352 E 2nd St in LA. Parking is relatively cheap. Don't go South into Skid Row.
